About

Not just a service provider.
Someone who's built real products.

I didn't start at a whiteboard. I started on the ground — managing projects, coordinating vendors, watching execution break at the last mile because the right tools didn't exist. That gap became my obsession.

From Project Manager to Web Developer to Co-founder, every step was deliberate. Today I run VendorMate, India's B2B contractor marketplace, and GyanSync, a digital agency delivering real results for businesses across India.
