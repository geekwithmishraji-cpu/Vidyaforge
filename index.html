About

Not just building products.
Building systems that scale.

My career didn't start at a whiteboard. It started on the ground — managing projects, coordinating vendors, watching execution fail at the last mile because the right tools didn't exist. That gap became my obsession.

From Project Manager to Web Developer to Co-founder, every step was deliberate. I didn't chase job titles — I chased problems worth solving. At Global Lancers, I learned how complex B2B workflows break down. At Veda Junction, I learned how to build digital systems people actually use. Then I decided to build one of my own.

Today, as Co-founder & CTO of VendorMate, I'm building India's B2B execution infrastructure — connecting corporate clients with verified ground teams at scale. The mission is simple: make India's MSME ecosystem trustworthy, trackable, and tech-enabled.

I think in systems. I ship in sprints. And I believe the best product is one that makes itself necessary.
